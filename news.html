Disney Channel's "The Owl House" Cancelled
The creator of Disney Channel's "The Owl House" has released an official statement regarding why the show was cancelled. According to her, the show was cut short because the show was attracting an audience that did not fit the disney brand. Rather than attracting an audience of 6 to 11-year-olds, the fanbase consisted of 18 to 25-year-olds.

Rick Riordan Announces new Percy Jackson Book
Last week, Rick Riordan confirmed that another book taking place in the Percy Jackson universe is currently in the works. The new book, which does not yet have a title, will follow Will Solace and Nico Di Angelo as they travel to Tartarus to free Bob the Titan. The book is not set to release until the summer of 2023.

DC League of Super Pets Announces Cast
During this Saturday's DC Fandome, DC announced that the following actors and actresses will be in the 3D animated movie: "DC League of Super Pets". The cast includes Dwayne Johson (Krypto), Kevin Hart (Ace), Kate McKinnon, John Krasinski, and Keanu Reeves.
